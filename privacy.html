Privacy Policy

This extension, K-Report User Blocker, does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

What Data We Access

The extension uses Chrome's local storage (chrome.storage.local) to store your preferences, such as blocked usernames and display options. This data stays on your device and is not shared with anyone.

Third-Party Data

The extension modifies the display of content on K-Report.net for your convenience, but it does not interact with any external services or APIs.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any user data. No analytics or tracking mechanisms are included.

Changes

This policy may be updated in the future if new features are added. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
